Guide: SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion Trial

Summary

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to set up and access the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion trial system.

Steps to Set Up SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion Trial

Step 1: Open the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion Trial Page

Open the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion trial page at https://www.sap.com/products/s4hana-erp/trial.html.

Step 2: Create an Account

Click on the Try now button. You will be redirected to the SAP Cloud Platform trial registration page. Fill in the required information to create an account.

Step 3: Registration Confirmation

Once your account is created, you will receive an email with the trial system details or direct access to the trial system with pop-up message on the screen.

Follow the instructions in the email to access the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edtion trial system.

Email Delivery

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact the SAP support team.
Trial System Availability

Trial systems are available for a limited time and may have restrictions on usage. You can extend the trial period by following again the steps to create a new trial account.
